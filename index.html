About YPSF

Creating brighter futures, together

Youth Publications & Socioeconomic Forum (YPSF) is a youth-driven NGO dedicated to advancing inclusive education and decent work. We convene young leaders, researchers, educators, employers, and public institutions to co-create solutions that improve lives.

Our model blends policy advocacy, publications, and hands-on programs. We elevate youth perspectives in global and local decision-making and invest in projects that show real-world results.

  • Inclusive governance and transparent decision-making
  • Open collaboration with networks and partner NGOs
  • Ethical standards, safeguarding, and data privacy

Our Mission

To amplify youth voices through research, publications, and action that advances quality education and decent work.

Our Vision

A world where young people shape policy and opportunity in every community.

Core Values

Integrity • Inclusion • Evidence • Collaboration • Impact.
